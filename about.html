About Me

Neal Shandilya

I am a 16 year old student from Howard County, Maryland, passionate about mathematics, physics, computer science, and building at a high level. I have been doing strict olympiad math training for years, studying physics seriously, and learning programming languages for as long as I can remember.

I am an aficionado of problem solving, creativity, and logic.

What sets me apart

At my school, I am the first student to achieve USAMO qualification, USAPhO qualification, USACO Platinum, and a 1600 SAT. I am also ranked #1 in the county (olympiad wise, no one surpasses me) and top 3 in Maryland. Those milestones reflect not just talent, but years of consistency, discipline, and a willingness to go deeper than what is normally expected. I've made studying and olympiads my life for the past year, and am ready to share what I learned with my students.

What truly sets me apart is the system behind the results. I’ve spent the past year breaking down how high-level problem solving actually works: how to think, how to approach unfamiliar problems, and how to stay consistent under pressure. My goal is not just to show students what to do, but to train them to think at an elite level on their own.

What I care about

I care about closing the gap between potential and access.

Too many students start behind because they lack exposure to the right guidance early on. I personally never had this mentor from the beginning and had to learn everything I know on my own. I am here to make sure you have the resources I wish I had once I started my academic journey.

I’m currently writing a complete introduction to the USA Mathematical Olympiad, designed to take students from AMC 8 / early competition math all the way to USAMO-level thinking. The full book will be released on this site for free (expected December 2026).

Everything I build—whether it's teaching, research, or writing—is driven by the same idea: talent should never be limited by access.

Beyond the numbers

For me, achievement is not just about collecting accomplishments. I believe through long-term training, genuine curiosity, ambition, and presence that you elevate your thinking and accomplishments follow.
